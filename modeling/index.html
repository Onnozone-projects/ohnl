Modeling

Special place in my hart and workshop is for the scale modeling. Based on my maritime research the model work has become an artistic exression. I do love to model classic passenger ships in scales of 1:200 and 1:400.

My intention is to capture the essence, the main design idea of the builders. Those models has unique minimalistic style and exceptinally accurate proportions. My passion is in this style that builders, designers and architects of passanger ships valued in their creative process of design.
